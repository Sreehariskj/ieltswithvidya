Our Courses

IELTS REGULAR ONLINE CLASS

This course is for those who have never prepared for IELTS test before and thereby would like to start from the basics.

COURSE STRUCTURE
  • Training in all four modules
  • How to answer each type of question in listening and reading.
  • Scoring criteria for writing and speaking.
  • How to structure different types of essays.
  • How to think of relevant ideas and examples in writing and speaking.
  • How to speak fluently without grammatical errors.

IELTS CUSTOMIZED PRIVATE CLASS

This course is a one-on-one class for those who have prepared for IELTS test before and need individual training on selected modules.

COURSE STRUCTURE
  • Individual one-on-one classes.
  • Students can choose the modules in which they prefer more training.
  • One hour session per day.
  • Extensive writing and speaking sessions if needed.
